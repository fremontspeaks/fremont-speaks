REVIEW GUIDELINES

Welcome to our website! If you are leaving a review, we ask that you leave an honest and respectful review from your experience with a teacher or administrator. We ask that you focus on their policies, grading, communication, and your overall experience. Please do not use inappropriate or discriminatory language, spread rumours, or bully anyone. These actions are unacceptable and completely discouraged. Reviews that violate these guidelines are at risk of being removed. Thank you!
